Anuradhapura

................

Anuradhapura is one of the olden capitals of Sri Lanka, well-known for its well-preserved ruins of ancient Lankan people. From the 4th century BC, it was the capital of Sri Lanka until the start of the 11th century AD. During this period it remained one of the most steady and durable centers of political power and city life in South Asia.
